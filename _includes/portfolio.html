Who Are We?

William Khaziri

William Khaziri
William Khaziri is the co-founder and CEO of Soundseed. He serves on the Advisory Board of Realstir Inc., a San Diego based real estate web search portal. Also, he oversees acquisitions and development for CHWD, his family’s real estate development firm. William is also a film producer, with his latest project Locked in Stone winning the acclaimed IndieFEST award.

David Khaziri

David Khaziri
David Khaziri is co-founder and Head of Research and Development for SoundSeed. As an Award Winning Film Director and Screenwriter, he brings a unique perspective to technology innovation with his storytelling experience. In addition, he attends Harvard University, pursuing a degree in Philosophy.

Jen Nguyen

Jen Nguyen
For Jen, User Experience is not just a career but a way of life. It's become a habit for her to observe the world and see ways in which it could be improved. She received a B.S in Cognitive Science with specialization in Human Computer Interactions at UCSD.

Timothy Griffith

Tim Griffith
Tim is co-founder and COO of Soundseed. In addition, he runs a freelance web development practice and is a contributing member of the development team for a San Diego based startup, SmartGarden. Tim received two B.A degrees in Political Science and Economics from UCSD in 2015.
